About Our Project

The Smart Irrigation System is designed to help farmers efficiently manage water resources by automating irrigation using modern technology. With real-time soil moisture, humidity, and rain detection, farmers can make informed decisions about irrigation.

Our system integrates IoT technology to provide real-time data and notifications, ensuring crops receive optimal hydration while conserving water resources.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide an intelligent and automated irrigation solution that enhances agricultural productivity while minimizing water wastage. Through our web portal and sensor-based alerts, we empower farmers with crucial information for better crop management.

By utilizing cutting-edge technology, we aim to revolutionize traditional farming methods, making agriculture more sustainable and resource-efficient.

Challenges Faced by Farmers

Farmers in many regions struggle with unpredictable rainfall, leading to severe irrigation problems. Water scarcity, high electricity costs, and outdated irrigation methods contribute to reduced crop yields and financial distress.

In recent years, thousands of farmers have suffered losses due to inefficient irrigation techniques, and in extreme cases, financial burdens have led to unfortunate farmer suicides. Our system aims to reduce these risks by providing efficient, data-driven irrigation solutions.

Project Functionalities

  • Automated irrigation system based on real-time soil moisture data.
  • Weather API integration for accurate weather predictions.
  • IoT-based sensors for humidity, temperature, and rainfall detection.
  • Web portal for farmers to monitor and control irrigation settings.
  • Alerts and notifications for critical conditions like excessive dryness or flooding.
  • Data logging and analysis for improved irrigation efficiency.
  • User-friendly dashboard for real-time monitoring.
  • Energy-efficient operation to reduce electricity costs.
  • Support for multiple crop types and soil conditions.
